WHAT IS THE SOSF?

The Special Operations Strike Force or SOSF strives to be an active roleplaying BRM5 Faction. Our members are expected to act with some degree of professionalism and we strive to become the best of the best. We try to find a balance between Roleplay, Grinding and of course having FUN!

The SOSF was founded on the 15th of January 2022 by @SupposedBrit and @OfficialRobloxDoge. We founded the SOSF because we believe brm5 is more fun with friends.

A detailed guide to the SOSF can be found here (WIP).

WANT TO JOIN?

You have to:

  • Be over thirteen years old.
  • Have Discord.
  • Speak English.
  • Have at least one star.
  • Have a basic understanding of the game.

If you think you're up for the challenge, please fill in this form. We'll contact you when you're accepted.

OUR APPLICATION PROCESS

  1. Application (Google Forms) review
  2. Try Out
  3. Screening
  4. Training
  5. ACCEPTED

We don't accept people which live in timezones that are radically diffrent to GMT, this is to keep our faction easy to manage.
The try out isn't really of much importance, and is just meant to evaluate your current skills.

NEW TO THE GAME?

We only accept people with one or more stars, this is to keep the faction easy to manage and train. We may start a private training program for new players in the future. Until then, we recommend that new players read the tutorials on the unofficial wiki. These tutorials can help a lot when trying to learn the game, as it is quite hard for beginners.
